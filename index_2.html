Job postings across Australia

We can see that the job postings are more populated in the capital cities, especially those in the south east of Australia. The density of population is high only within the metropolitan cities that are highlighted by the bubbles, which explains the density of jobs also being greater in the same cities. Therefore, living in one of these developed cities provides a clear advantage to gaining job opportunities
